About the Ballon d'Or

History & Origin

The Ballon d'Or, French for "Golden Ball," was established in 1956 by France Football magazine. Initially, it was awarded to the best European player, but in 1995 it was expanded to include players from any origin playing for European clubs. In 2007, it became a global award, recognizing the world's best player regardless of nationality or club.
